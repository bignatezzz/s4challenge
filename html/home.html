Welcome to CompeteWithMe

Hello there. Welcome to CompeteWithMe website made for people of all ages who might be bored and not very motivated to be active, happy, and have fun during this global pandemic. This website is totally free of cost and is open to all ages. We hope this website will help all to stay fit, healthy, and happy.

~ONLINE SAFETY~ We are very concerned about online safety. To ensure you, we will never ask for any personal information or any other information. Your child will only have to make up a username such as (Unipowergirl) and make a random password to log in.
