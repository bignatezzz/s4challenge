Welcome to Compete With Me

Hello there, FUN4KIDS is a website made for kids who might be bored during this global pandemic. We know how hard it is for parents to manage a job and a bored child. We hope this website will help kids get over their boredom in a fun way! This website is totally free of cost and is open to all ages. We hope this website will help you and your child have fun during these days we are stuck at home.

~SAFETY~ We are very concerned about online safety. To ensure you, we will never ask for any personal information or any other information. Your child will only have to make up a username such as (Unipowergirl) and make a random password to log in
